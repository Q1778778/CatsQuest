The following is the structure of enemy.json.

name: string (*name of an enemy*)

description: string ("description of an ememy")

HP: int (*total health of an enemy*)

level: int (*level of an ememy*)

experience: int (*the experience can be gained after defeating one enemy*)

strength: int (*an effective damage to the player's health*)
